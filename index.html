Modern Roof Consulting

Modern Roof Consulting, Inc. is an independent, full service Roof Consulting firm, specializing in all types of flat/steep roofs. We work directly for you, as an added staff member, to help you determine needs and find solutions to roofing issues. We are not affiliated with any manufacturer, contractor or supplier. From overall Roof Master Plans, to leak investigation, we do it all. Talk to us about; Bid Documents, Project Management, Quality Assurance Observation, Performance Contracts.
